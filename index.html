About Me


My name is Andrew.

I am an engineering physicist, amateur videographer, and aspiring polymath.

I enjoy working on complex and chaotic multidisiplinary problems, with a focus on the intersection of physics, engineering, and computer science. My current interests include nuclear fusion, brain-computer interfaces, and machine learning.


I've interned at General Fusion, on both the Systems Engineering and Controls Engineering teams, and at Tesla, on the Sensors team. I'll be starting at Neuralink after I graduate as an Electrical Engineer on the Surgery Robotics team.

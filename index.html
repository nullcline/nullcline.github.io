About Me


My name is Andrew.

I am an engineer, physicist, and trace amounts of everything else.

I enjoy working on complex and chaotic multidisiplinary problems, with a focus on the intersection of physics, engineering, and computer science. My current interests include nuclear fusion, brain-computer interfaces, and machine learning.

I currently attend the University of British Columbia, where I am pursuing a degree in Engineering Physics.

I've previously interned at General Fusion, on both the Systems Engineering and Controls Engineering teams, as well as at Tesla, on the Sensors team.
